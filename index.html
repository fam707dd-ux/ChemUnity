The Team

The people behind ChemUnity — and the roles still open.

Strategic Council

The Strategic Council is ChemUnity’s long-term guidance body. It is not involved in daily execution or event operations. Instead, members focus on protecting the direction, standards, and sustainability of the organization as it grows.

Responsibilities include:

  • Defining long-term vision and direction
  • Ensuring academic and ethical integrity
  • Reviewing organizational progress at a high level
  • Supporting sustainable growth and structure
  • Participating in key strategic decisions when needed
This role is NOT for you if you want to: Run events, manage tasks, create weekly content, or work in hands-on execution roles.

Fieldwork Division

The Fieldwork Division is the active working structure of ChemUnity. This is where ideas become output — through research, content, events, and collaboration. Members are expected to contribute consistently and work within a structured, goal-driven environment.

Core areas include:

  • Research & Content Development
  • Outreach & External Collaboration
  • Events & Webinar Execution
  • Design, Media & Communication
  • Community Support & Coordination
  • Flexible volunteer contributions when needed

What to expect:

  • Clear weekly responsibilities
  • Real deliverables and accountability
  • Team-based execution of projects
  • A professional, structured workflow
